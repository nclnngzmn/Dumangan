Dumangan

God of Good Harvest

Dumangan is the god of good harvest, people praise him to have a good and beautiful harvest as well as better quality of rice. While planting or harvesting crops, farmers would sing Dumangan praises and hymns. Alongside with Dumangan, he also has siblings Kalasakas, Kalasokus, and Damulag who were just as powerful as him.

Our Story

This website was created by the students of Adamson University. This platform aims to help the local farmers to earn more and increase their profit by promoting their crops on the said online web platform so that they could keep up with the products from the urban areas rather than for people to patronize imported products that also exist in the country with the same usage and satisfaction
